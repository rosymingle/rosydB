about

Rosy (Rory McDougall) is a skilled multi-instrumental producer and performer with 20+ years experience in live performance, composition and music production. Known for blending high-level musicianship with cabaret flair, Rosy thrives in high-energy, immersive, and queer-friendly performance environments. Adept on bass, drums, guitar and backing vocals with a keen ear for arrangement and an eye for the stage. Ready to bring rock grit and technical finesse to your sound.

Genres: rock, folk, roots, reggae, ska, punk, grunge, prog, soul, jazz, blues, pop, etc.

Instruments: bass (electric & upright), drums, guitar, ukulele, keys.
