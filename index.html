services

  • Full-Stack Production – From a rough idea to a radio-ready masterpiece.
  • Studio Tracking – High-fidelity recording in a space that doesn’t sound like a cardboard box.
  • Creative Arrangement – Fixing your "bridge to nowhere" and making your choruses pop.
  • Session Performance – Need bass, drums, or guitar? I’ve got the chops, and the gear.
  • Custom Backing Tracks – Professional arrangements for solo artists who need a "band in a box".
  • Mastering – Making sure your tracks hit hard on streaming platforms and.

styles

  • Indie & Alternative – From jangly guitar pop to gritty, garage-bred rock.
  • Blues, Roots & Jazz – Rough cut roots and catchy, upbeat cabaret hits.
  • Prog & Psych – Big fuzz, spacey echoes, and taming the beautiful chaos.
  • Singer-Songwriter – Intimate, "breathable" acoustic sessions that capture the rawness and honesty.
  • Post-Punk & New Wave – Angular guitars and driving basslines for that moody midnight vibe.
  • Pop & Commercial – Polished, punchy, and ready to dominate the airwaves.

gear

The Brain (DAW & Interface)

  • Logic Pro & Ableton Live – The best of both worlds for composition and chaos.
  • Universal Audio Interface – Pristine conversion with that sweet analog emulation.
  • Stompboxes galore – An array of effects that cost more than my car.

The Soul (Instruments)

  • The "Holy Trinity" – Professional Grade Electric & Acoustic Guitars, Basses, and a Studio Drum Kit.
  • Analog & Soft Synths – From lush pads to bass that’ll rattle your neighbor's windows.
  • The Pedalboard – A curated collection of boutique fuzz, delay, and "magic sauce."

The Ears (Mics & Monitoring)

  • Condenser & Dynamic Mics – Selected for their character and versatility.
  • Professional Monitoring – Flat-response monitors and open-back headphones so I hear the truth, even when it hurts.
  • Acoustic Treatment – Deadened surfaces because the only "echoey" thing should be the reverb.
