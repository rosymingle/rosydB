about

Rosy (Rory McDougall) is a skilled multi-instrumental producer and performer with 20+ years experience in live performance, composition and music production. Known for blending high-level musicianship with cabaret flair, Rosy thrives in high-energy, immersive, and queer-friendly performance environments. Adept on bass, drums, guitar and backing vocals with a keen ear for arrangement and an eye for the stage. Ready to bring rock grit and technical finesse to your sound.

Genres: rock, folk, roots, reggae, ska, punk, grunge, prog, soul, jazz, blues, pop, etc.

Instruments: bass (electric & upright), drums, guitar, ukulele, keys.

services

  • Full-Stack Production – From a rough idea to a radio-ready masterpiece.
  • Studio Tracking – High-fidelity recording in a space that doesn’t sound like a cardboard box.
  • Creative Arrangement – Fixing your "bridge to nowhere" and making your choruses pop.
  • Composition – Brass, woodwind or keys, whatever your song needs.
  • Score – Need parts on paper? I'll scribe and deliver via Noteflight.
  • Session Performance – Need bass, drums or guitar? I’ve got the chops, and the gear.

  • Mastering – So your tracks hit hard on streaming platforms.
  • Live Production – Capture a polished multitrack recording of your show.
  • Live Tech – Need a soundie? I got you.
