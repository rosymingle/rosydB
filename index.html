about

Rosy (Rory McDougall) is a skilled multi-instrumental producer and performer with 20+ years’ experience in live performance, composition and music production. Known for blending high-level musicianship with cabaret flair, Rosy thrives in high-energy, immersive, and queer-friendly performance environments. Adept on guitar, bass, drums, and backing vocals with a keen ear for arrangement and an eye for the stage. Ready to bring rock n’ roll grit and technical finesse to your sound.

services

  • Full-Stack Production – From a rough idea to a radio-ready masterpiece.
  • Studio Tracking – High-fidelity recording in a space that doesn’t sound like a cardboard box.
  • Creative Arrangement – Fixing your "bridge to nowhere" and making your choruses pop.
  • Session Performance – Need bass, drums or guitar? I’ve got the chops, and the gear.
  • Custom Backing Tracks – Professional arrangements for solo artists who want a "band in a box".
  • Mastering – Making sure your tracks hit hard on streaming platforms and.

styles

  • Indie & Alternative – From jangly guitar pop to gritty, garage-bred rock.
  • Blues, Roots & Jazz – Rough cut roots and catchy, upbeat cabaret hits.
  • Prog & Psych – Big fuzz, spacey echoes, and taming the beautiful chaos.
  • Singer-Songwriter – Intimate, "breathable" acoustic sessions that capture the rawness and honesty.

  • Pop & Commercial – Polished, punchy, and ready to dominate the airwaves.
